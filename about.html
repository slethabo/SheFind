ABOUT US

FOR WOMEN BY WOMEN

"She Finds, with its empowering slogan 'She is AI,' is a dynamic web platform dedicated to the advancement and impact of women in the field of Artificial Intelligence. This platform serves as a powerful connector, encouraging women to register and discover a range of AI-related opportunities from leadership roles to community memberships. Our primary objective is to inspire women to join these AI groups, fostering a space for them to share their passion through talks, discussions, and by offering their unique expertise. In essence, She Finds amplifies the presence of women within the AI community, promoting their contributions and creating a ripple effect of influence. Remember, our platform is more than just a tool—it's a movement aimed at creating an engaging and diverse AI community."
